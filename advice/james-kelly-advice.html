Hi, my name is James and I have some advice for you!

Embrace the challenge

If it was easy it would become boring and meaningless, challenge is what grows you and makes you wiser.

To stay positive, set smaller goals, celebrate when you achieve them, and express gratitude often.

Fake it til you make it.

Most of life is fake it till you make it. You do the best you can, you screw up, you learn lessons, and you repeat. Eventually you get really good.
