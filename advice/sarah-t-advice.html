Everyone's Experience is Different

If there's one thing I've learned in life it's that everyone's experience is different. Just because someone had a bad experience, didn't like their job, couldn't find a job, etc doesn't mean that will be your story. People will tell you things all the time, and you can listen but don't have to internalize. What works or didn't work for someone else isn't what will or will not work for you.
