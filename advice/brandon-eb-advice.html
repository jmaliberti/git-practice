Hi, my name is Brandon E-B and I have some advice for you!

Stick with it

When you first start programming, it will probably be a little confusing and challenging...

But be sure to stick with it! Things will start to click, and it'll feel great when you accomplish new goals!

Experiment

There's no need to rush through lessons and projects. Feel free to experiment: try new things, mess around, take things apart and see if you can put them back together again! Doing is a huge part of learning!
