Hi, my name is Sharon and I have some advice for you!

Organize

Normally, I have advice I give away freely because I just do not use it. Coding is on another level, however. I will share some of my tips and tricks but, at the end of the day, it is really what works for you.

One, I take notes to stay organized and to have a reference. I also have a three-ring binder for printouts and "cheat sheets" that Skillcrush provides. Having resources in one or two spots saves time and lowers frustration levels.

Time Management

Two, set some dedicated time to work on the class. One or two hours a few days a week is very beneficial. If you have more time, go for it. Also, finish the section you start in one sitting. It helps with your flow and your sanity.

Three, use Google if you are stuck. The answer is always available when you ask the question properly.

Finally, have fun. It is truly a wonderful adventure. You got this1
