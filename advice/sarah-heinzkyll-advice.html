Hi, my name is Sarah and I have some advice for you!

It's all about the balance!

When I get really into learning a new, interesting skill I might be so into it that I might lose myself in the intent to finish all the modules in one evening.

I might lose touch with feelings like thirst or hunger and just want to solve the next problem.


Can't stop!

Remind myself of rest, air, food and movement.

I sometimes even put an alarm to interrupt my work flow and take care of my physical needs and go for a walk and look at, or interact with plants, animals or any living creature.

During these processes I need to get to know myself and find out whats challenging for me and what can support me in it.

Putting on my favourite clothes, chosing colours or aroma oils, jumping for 10 minutes- I want to find out what does me well and just do it. Which is not easy. hahahah
